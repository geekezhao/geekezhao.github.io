Meta Analysis

2022-04-10 Notes Comments Word Count: 1.2k(words) Read Count: 5(minutes)

Google的一个简单印象

What is Meta Analysis?

ref

A meta-analysis is a type of statistical analysis where the results of multiple studies are combined and then analyzed.

Why is Meta Analysis Important?

A meta-analysis is helpful because it’s a review designed to summarize information. It follows a few general principles in that a meta-analysis:

  • Is done systematically
  • Follows certain criteria
  • Contains a pool of results
  • Is based on a quantitative analysis

Quantitative analysis involves using mathematical and statistical techniques to measure, model, and understand aspects of human behavior.

Where a single study has more limitations, a meta-analysis provides more robust results that can help psychology researchers better understand the magnitude and of an effect.

Meta-Analysis “Increases” Sample Size.
One of the reasons why meta-analyses are so useful is because of an all too common problem across many research studies: small sample sizes.

A Guide to Conduct Meta Analysis

Comparison between ‘Narrative Review’, ‘Systematic Review’, and ‘Meta Analysis’

Narrative Review: 这种是文章中捎带的LR

  • 文章是人为选择的，根据每个研究的主题只选取一类主题的文章
  • 在每篇文章中只选择“作者想说的话”
  • 这种综述是经验性的，定性的，并没有合理的量化工具（主要是无法比较论文间的结果）
  • 综述并不关心样本特征只关心实验结果

Systematic Review: 这种应该就是单独写一篇的综述

  • Systematic review ensures that the search strategies are replicable.(可重复性)

Meta Analysis:

  • In a meta-analysis, researchers focus not on statistical significance of individual studies, but on the magnitude of the effect or effect size. (后文中详细叙述，侧重在effect size的比较)

归根结底，两种比较好的综述方法的区别在于：

  • predefined criteria <=> systematic review
  • effect size & unbiased research <=> meta analysis

When to Conduct?

  • Enough primary research

    • enough不指数量，具体多少算enough需要根据引用文章的bias大小，cover的变量数目、变量关系来确定
    • One common approach to estimate the number of studies required is based on power analysis.（Power Analysis是一种确认primary research数目的工具。）

  • Essential to conduct

    • Topic非常热门
    • Topic下的研究过多需要一个meta analysis重新梳理方向

How to Conduct? (What Are the Common Effect Sizes?)

Meta Analysis的根本就是对于effect size的比较，所以主要的方法也是讨论unbiased比较的方法。

什么样的feature或者说指标可以放到meta analysis进行比较？
标准的和非标准化处理的feature都可以放入比较，但需要一些可以横向比较的指标，是明确的，有方向性，有大小，至少不是抽象的。（比如pesudo-Rsquare绝不可以放进来，但coefficient就可以。）

不同的feature如何比较？

简而言之，feature在此处分为三类：

  • 二元组间ratio比较（男女、treatment和对照 —> odds ratio）
  • 组间均值比较
  • coefficient比较 —> 需要用fisher’s Z score标准化

如何选择measure effect size的model？

  • Fixed effect model
  • Random effect model

其间差异在于是否考虑不同研究之间样本选取的不同，fixed effect认为所有的研究样本量是一致的，random effect认为每个研究样本选取都会带来bias需要增加一个与研究相关的变量控制。关键在于whether effect sizes are homogeneous across studies，这个问题可以用Q-statistic解决。

How to Conduct and Interpret Moderator Analysis?

主要用来解释研究之间effect size不同，是有什么造成的呢，是因为sample的选取不同嘛？mixed-effects meta-analysis可以用于解决这个问题。（这个回归只是最简单的线性回归，但结果解释非常tricky。）

举例如下，meta analysis研究的对象永远只是“research”而不是primary research里的individual。如果进行了“研究层面到个体层面”的错误推断，这种错误被称为“The Ecological Fallacy”。

What Are the Common Pitfalls ofMeta-Analysis and their Solutions?

The presence of publication bias may affect the validity of the meta analysis.

  • the apples and orange problem.
  • garbage in, garbage out
  • extracted effect sizes are likely correlated or non-independent.
